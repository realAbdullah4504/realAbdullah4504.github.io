# CopyFlow — School Submission & Print Workflow Management System

## Summary

CopyFlow is a role-based school administration platform digitizing lesson submission workflows, print instructions, and administrative review. It replaces paper-based tracking with a centralized four-role system where teachers submit lessons, principals review schedules, secretaries manage print queues, and administrators oversee operations—automating document lifecycle and calendar-driven visibility.
