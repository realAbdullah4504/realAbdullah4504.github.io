# Nutrifactor Mobile Commerce & Customer Experience Platform

A mobile-first commerce platform that provides a complete shopping experience for health and wellness products through a native mobile application. It solves fragmented mobile e-commerce by streamlining product discovery, purchasing workflows, and customer engagement via Shopify's commerce infrastructure. Customers can browse products, manage carts, complete purchases, maintain wishlists, and receive personalized recommendations through a unified mobile experience optimized for retention and conversion.
